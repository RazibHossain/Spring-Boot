About ThaiWonder Tours

Passionate about Thailand. Dedicated to unforgettable journeys.

Our Story

Founded in 2012, ThaiWonder Tours was born from a deep love for Thailand and a belief that everyone deserves to experience its magic. What started as a small Bangkok-based operation has grown into one of Thailand's most trusted tour companies, with over 10,000 happy travelers across 60+ countries.

Our team is made up of passionate local Thai guides, travel experts, and hospitality professionals who are dedicated to crafting experiences that go beyond the typical tourist trail.

Our Mission

To make Thailand accessible to every traveler — regardless of budget — while supporting local communities, promoting sustainable tourism, and delivering world-class experiences.
